Store Pick-Up

Store pick-up is a delivery option that lets buyers collect their purchased item(s) from a physical store instead of the conventional delivery method. Store pick-up provides buyers with an additional option in receiving their parcel(s) with zero shipping fees.

NOTE: Store pick-up is only available for certain stores.
You can check the store locations which provides store pick-up service here.



Buying Store Pick-Up Items

You will be able to select Store Pick-Up Option upon Checkout, and choose your preferred physical store to collect from.

NOTE: No shipping fee will be incurred for store pick-up items.



Common Questions

1. What if I cannot go to the store within 14 days?

You may ask a representative to collect on your behalf. Do provide them with the QR code, so that the staff will be able to verify your order. If the QR code is wrong/not provided, you will not able to collect the order. If neither you nor your representative is able to go to the store within the timeframe, the order will be automatically cancelled and the cost will be refunded to you.


2. Can I change my delivery method after selecting store pick-up?

No, once your order is confirmed, you won't be able to change to a different delivery method. If you'd like to do so, you can cancel your existing order and place a new one to change the delivery method.

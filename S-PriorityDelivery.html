Priority Delivery

Priority Delivery is a delivery service option where your orders will be prioritized for a delivery and you will receive your parcel (s) faster compare to the Standard Delivery option.


Estimated Delivery Time

You can expect to receive your parcel (s) within 1-3 working days from the time your orders are placed successfully.

For the orders that are placed on a weekday before 8pm, the parcel (s) will either be delivered on the following working day or the day after.
